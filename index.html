Beef mince pasta with pesto

Ingredients

  • 900g of beef mince (of your own choice)
  • 500g of Durum pasta
  • 350g of pesto (of your own choice)
  • 2 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 2 big carrots
  • 1 red onion
  • 30g of olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, onion powder to season
  • Grated hard cheese (optional)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Boil pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Cut carrots, garlic, red onion to small pieces and cook until golden and set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Add beef mince, then seasoning and cook until golden brown.
  5. Add garlic, red onion, carrots and cook for 1 minute.
  6. Pour in pasta and pesto mix it well and let it simmer for 3–4 minutes.
  7. Ater that serve it with any grated hard cheese of your own choice
